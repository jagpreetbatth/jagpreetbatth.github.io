Python, Jupyter & Excel Project: Instacart Online Grocery Insights

Problem: An online grocery company, instacart is in need of exploratory data analysis to determine temporal sales patterns, customer purchasing habits, and department trends in order to create a targeted marketing campaign.

Solution: Utilizing core data analytic methods, we wrangle, merge, aggregate and derive data values we visualized trends, forecasts and customer preferences to aid in profit driven marketing. For example, by studying time periods that customers spent the most money, we could initiate “flash sales” during slow sales time periods to increase customer spending leading to increased ROIs.

Tools: Jupyter Notebook, Excel Macros, Python coding and visualizations

Excel - Tableau Project: Preparing for Influenza Season

Problem: A medical staffing agency covering U.S. hospitals requires a retrospective data analysis study in order to gain insight for temporary healthcare worker placement for the upcoming Influenza season.

Solution: Using core data analytic tools, we profile, clean and use statistical data to forecast the need for staff in geographical locations with the most deaths related to the Influenza virus, to ensure lives are saved in the future. This project used insight from the past in order to prepare for the future, giving hospitals the correct number of staff to decrease expenditures and direct sources where they are most needed.

Tools: Microsoft Excel, Powerpoint and Tableau

Python-Jupyter-Plotly Project: COVID-19 Response
